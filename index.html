Our Mission

WinSAT's CubeSat division is tasked to design, test, and build a 3U Cube Satellite for Low Earth Orbit (LEO). Competing in CSDC-5, WinSAT is tasked with developing a "Selfie-Sat" a CubeSat which will take a "space selfie" photo when commanded by an amateur radio station. Amateur ("ham") radio operators will be able to command Selfie-Sat to take a "space-selfie" when overhead, and immediately downlink it for viewing. Schools, science centers, universities, or conferences - anywhere in the world - will be able to host interesting, inspiring, and educational space-selfie events. Team Divisions

Attitude Determination and Control System

Attitude control is controlling the orientation of an object with respect to an inertial frame of reference or another entity like the celestial sphere, certain fields, and nearby objects

Business

Gathering sponsors, marketing, and managing all financial operations.

Command and Data Handling

Management of all forms of data on the spacecraft including acting upon recieved data from Earth, transmitting data, and managing all subsystems. This system is composed mainly of the space flight computer, the flight software, and the solid state recorder.

Earth Observation

Design of instruments for collecting data about the physical, chemical, and biological system of the planet Earth using remote-sensing technologies.

Electric Power System

The management of obtaining power, storing energy, distributing power, power regulation and control. Design of primary and secondary batteries and photovoltaic power generators.

Payload

Design of the camera sensors and lens.

Structural

Computer aided design of the spacecraft and simulation of mechanical systems.

Thermal

Thermal sensor design and systems simulations.

Web Development

Creation and management of the codebase for the team website.
